Champagne, Swords, and Braised Meats.

Never has this combination of things made more sense than at Sabre.

Sabre's menu features the perfect pairing of small plates with sparkling wine, including oysters, homemade cheese and cured meats, and whole roasted pig. A prix fixe menu is offered on a nightly basis.

Are you finding the current cork-twisting method of opening bottles of bubbly a bit anticlimactic? Sabre offers tableside lessons in the art of sabrage, the fiercest way to pop champagne.
